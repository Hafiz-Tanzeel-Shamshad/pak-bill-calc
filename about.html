About PakBillCalc

PakBillCalc is a simple and practical website built for Pakistani households who want to estimate their electricity bill before it arrives. We use NEPRA 2026 base tariff rates and commonly published charges to create clear, easy to understand estimates. Our goal is not to replace your official DISCO bill, but to help you plan monthly budgets and avoid surprises.

Electricity bills are complicated. There are slabs, surcharges, and adjustments that change each month. We break down the total into energy charges, GST, income tax, and other line items, so you can see where your money goes. Our calculators cover all major DISCOs, including LESCO, MEPCO, IESCO, FESCO, GEPCO, PESCO, HESCO, SEPCO, QESCO, and K-Electric.

We also publish practical guides on tariff slabs, FCA, protected consumer rules, and solar savings. These guides are written in plain English for regular households, not engineers. We update content each year to keep it relevant and accurate for current tariffs.

The idea for PakBillCalc came from a simple observation: most people in Pakistan receive their electricity bill and have no way to verify whether the charges are correct before the due date. By providing a free, transparent estimate that shows every charge line by line, we help households spot anomalies, plan usage around slab thresholds, and avoid late payment surprises. Our solar savings calculator was added in response to growing interest in rooftop solar across Pakistan.

We are committed to keeping the site free and accessible. There are no paywalls, no registration requirements, and no hidden fees. All calculators and guides are publicly available to anyone who needs them. We plan to expand with more DISCO-specific guides, seasonal tariff analysis, and interactive tools that help users compare billing periods.

If you have suggestions or spot an issue, feel free to contact us. We aim to improve the tools and keep them helpful for everyone. Thank you for using PakBillCalc.
