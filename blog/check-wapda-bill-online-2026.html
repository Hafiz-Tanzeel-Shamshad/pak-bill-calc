How to Check LESCO/MEPCO/WAPDA Bill Online - Step by Step 2026

By Hafiz Tanzeel Shamshad — Updated: May 20, 2026

Checking your electricity bill online is the fastest way to confirm the total before the printed bill arrives. The PITC portal supports most DISCOs, including LESCO, MEPCO, IESCO, FESCO, GEPCO, PESCO, HESCO, SEPCO, and QESCO. This guide explains how to use the portal, how to check by SMS, and what to do if the website is down.

After checking your bill, compare it with the estimate from the main calculator or your DISCO calculator to understand any differences.

Step 1: Find your reference number

Your reference number is printed on your previous bill, usually near the top. It is a 14 digit number that identifies your connection. You will need this to check your bill online.

Step 2: Open the PITC portal

Visit the PITC bill portal and select your DISCO. Enter your reference number and submit. The current bill will appear with the total amount, due date, and a PDF download option.

Step 3: Download or print

Once the bill is displayed, download the PDF for record keeping or print it if needed for payment. This is also the easiest way to obtain a duplicate bill if you did not receive the physical copy.

SMS method (if available)

Some DISCOs offer SMS bill checking. You send your reference number to a short code and receive the bill amount by SMS. Check your DISCO official website for the correct code and format. As of 2026, LESCO, MEPCO, IESCO, and FESCO have active SMS services for basic bill inquiries. The typical format is to type your 14-digit reference number and send it to the designated short code published on the DISCO website. SMS charges may apply depending on your mobile network.

Understanding your bill after checking online

Once you view your bill online, take a moment to review each line item. The bill shows your energy charges broken down by slab, fixed charges based on your sanctioned load, GST at 18%, income tax based on filer status, and surcharges like the Neelum Jhelum and financing cost charges. The FCA line shows the monthly fuel adjustment, which can be positive or negative. If you are unsure about any charge, enter the same units into the bill calculator on this site and compare the breakdown. This helps you spot errors before the payment due date. If the online bill shows units that do not match your meter reading, contact your DISCO customer service immediately.

Using DISCO mobile apps

Several DISCOs have official mobile apps. These can show bills, complaints, and service updates. Apps are useful if the PITC portal is slow or down.

What if the portal is not working?

Try again after some time or use a different browser. You can also check through the DISCO app or visit a customer service center. If you need an estimate urgently, use the calculator on this site and compare later.

FAQs

What is the PITC portal?

PITC is the official online portal for checking electricity bills of most DISCOs.

Do I need a reference number to check my bill?

Yes, you need the 14 digit reference number printed on your bill.

Can I check my bill by SMS?

Some DISCOs offer SMS bill checking; check their official instructions.

Can I download a duplicate bill online?

Yes, you can download a PDF copy from the PITC portal.

What if the portal is not working?

Try again later or use your DISCO app or customer service helpline.
