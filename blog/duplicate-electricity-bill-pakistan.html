What is a Duplicate Electricity Bill and How to Get One in Pakistan?

By Hafiz Tanzeel Shamshad — Updated: May 20, 2026

If you lost your printed bill or did not receive it, you can download a duplicate bill online. The duplicate bill is an official copy of your current bill and can be used for payment before the due date. This guide explains how to get a duplicate bill using the PITC portal and what to do if the portal is not working.

After downloading the bill, compare it with an estimate from the main calculator to confirm the charges and slab usage.

Step 1: Find your reference number

Your reference number is printed on any previous bill. It is a 14 digit number that identifies your connection. You will need it to access the duplicate bill online.

Step 2: Use the PITC portal

Open the PITC bill portal and select your DISCO. Enter the reference number and submit. The bill will appear on screen with an option to download or print. This PDF copy is your duplicate bill.

Step 3: Save or print the bill

Download the PDF and save it. You can pay using the duplicate bill at banks, online portals, or mobile wallets as long as it is within the due date.

Alternative methods

Some DISCOs have official mobile apps where you can view or download bills. If the PITC portal is down, try the DISCO app or visit your local customer service office for help.

DISCO-specific duplicate bill instructions

Each DISCO handles duplicate bills slightly differently. LESCO customers can use the LESCO online bill portal or the LESCO mobile app available on the Google Play Store. MEPCO, IESCO, and FESCO customers can also use their respective DISCO portals linked from the PITC main page. K-Electric customers have a separate portal on the KE website because KE follows a different billing system. For HESCO, SEPCO, and QESCO, the PITC portal is usually the most reliable option. PESCO customers in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa can also check bills through the PESCO official website. If you are unsure which portal to use, start with PITC and select your DISCO from the dropdown menu on the homepage.

What to do if the duplicate bill shows wrong charges

If the duplicate bill you downloaded shows charges that do not match your estimate from the bill calculator, investigate before paying. Check the unit count on the bill against your meter reading. If the units are higher than what your meter shows, the bill may be estimated rather than based on an actual reading. File a complaint with your DISCO immediately to request a corrected bill. You can compare your bill breakdown with our bill calculation guide to identify which charge seems incorrect. Common discrepancies include wrong consumer type classification, incorrect sanctioned load, or a missing FCA adjustment.

Common issues and fixes

If your bill does not appear, double check the reference number. Try again later if the portal is slow. If the bill is still missing, contact the DISCO helpline. Keep your CNIC and previous bill details ready for verification.

FAQs

What is a duplicate electricity bill?

A duplicate bill is a reprint of the current bill that you can download online.

How do I get a duplicate bill online?

Use the PITC portal and enter your reference number to view and download the bill.

Do I need to pay for a duplicate bill?

No, downloading a duplicate bill online is usually free.

Can I use the duplicate bill for payment?

Yes, the duplicate bill can be used for payment before the due date.

What if the portal does not show my bill?

Try again later or visit your DISCO office for assistance.
