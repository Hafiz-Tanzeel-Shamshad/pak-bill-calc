FESCO Bill Calculator 2026 - Faisalabad Electricity Rate Guide

By Hafiz Tanzeel Shamshad — Updated: May 20, 2026

FESCO serves Faisalabad and a large portion of central Punjab. This guide explains how FESCO bills are calculated, how to check your bill online, and how to estimate your monthly charges using NEPRA 2026 tariffs. For an immediate estimate, open the FESCO bill calculator.

FESCO coverage areas

FESCO covers Faisalabad, Sargodha, Jhang, Toba Tek Singh, and nearby districts. The billing structure follows the national NEPRA slab rates, so your total depends on your monthly units and consumer type.

How FESCO bills are calculated

The bill starts with energy charges based on slab rates. Then fixed charges are added based on your sanctioned load, followed by GST and income tax. Surcharges like the Neelum Jhelum and financing cost charges are included, and FCA can change monthly. To see a full breakdown, use the main calculator and compare the slab table with your bill.

How to check FESCO bill online

You can check your FESCO bill using the PITC portal. Enter your reference number to view and download the bill. The online bill guide explains the steps in detail.

Common reasons for high FESCO bills

Slab jumps are the most common reason. If you cross 200 or 300 units, the per-unit rate rises. Summer usage also increases units due to cooling. FCA adjustments and non-filer income tax can add more cost. Review the slab chart in the tariff guide to understand the impact.

Tips to reduce your FESCO bill

Use efficient appliances, reduce standby power usage, and shift heavy loads to off-peak hours. If you are close to protected status, keep your average below 200 units. For long term savings, explore rooftop solar. Use the solar savings calculator to estimate payback and the solar analysis guide for a deeper view.

FESCO complaints and support

If your bill seems incorrect, file a complaint and keep your complaint number. You can visit a local FESCO office or use helpline services. Our complaint guide explains what to do next.

FAQs

Which areas are covered by FESCO?

FESCO covers Faisalabad, Sargodha, Jhang, Toba Tek Singh, and nearby districts.

How can I check my FESCO bill online?

Use the PITC portal with your reference number to view or download your bill.

Why is my FESCO bill higher than expected?

Slab jumps, high summer usage, and FCA adjustments are common reasons.

Does FESCO follow NEPRA slabs?

Yes, FESCO follows NEPRA national slabs with standard charges.
