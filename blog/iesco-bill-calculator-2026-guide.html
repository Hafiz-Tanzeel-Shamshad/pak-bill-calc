IESCO Bill Calculator 2026 - Islamabad and Rawalpindi Tariff Guide

By Hafiz Tanzeel Shamshad — Updated: May 20, 2026

IESCO serves Islamabad, Rawalpindi, and nearby districts, making it one of the most visible DISCOs in Pakistan. This guide explains how IESCO bills are calculated, how to check your bill online, and how to estimate your charges using NEPRA 2026 tariffs. For a fast calculation, open the IESCO bill calculator and enter your monthly units.

IESCO coverage areas

IESCO covers Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Attock, Chakwal, and Jhelum. If your bill is issued by IESCO, the NEPRA slab rates apply in the same way as other DISCOs. The main difference is in local customer service and reporting channels, not the slab rates themselves.

How IESCO bills are calculated

IESCO bills follow the NEPRA 2026 slab system. Your units are divided into slabs based on your consumer type, and each slab is charged at a different rate. After energy charges, fixed charges, GST, income tax, and surcharges like NJ and financing cost are added. The FCA adjustment may vary monthly and can either increase or decrease the final bill. A clear breakdown is available in the main calculator.

How to check IESCO bill online

Use the PITC portal to check your bill online. Enter your reference number to view and download the bill. If you want a step by step guide, read how to check WAPDA bills online. Many users also keep a PDF copy for payment records.

Why IESCO bills rise suddenly

Slab jumps are the main reason. Crossing 200 or 300 units increases the per-unit rate, which increases the total. FCA adjustments and non-filer income tax can add additional increases. Compare your usage against the slab chart in our tariff guide if the bill looks unusually high.

Tips to reduce IESCO bills

Track your units during the month, reduce peak usage, and switch to efficient appliances. If you are close to the protected threshold, reduce usage to stay protected. For long term savings, evaluate rooftop solar using the solar savings calculator and read the solar cost vs savings analysis.

IESCO complaints and support

If you suspect incorrect readings or billing errors, file a complaint as early as possible. Keep the complaint number and a copy of your bill. Use our complaint guide for a full checklist.

FAQs

Which areas are covered by IESCO?

IESCO covers Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Attock, Chakwal, and Jhelum.

How can I check my IESCO bill online?

Use the PITC portal with your reference number to view or download your bill.

Does IESCO follow NEPRA slabs?

IESCO uses NEPRA national slabs with standard taxes and surcharges.

Why is my IESCO bill higher than expected?

Slab jumps, FCA changes, and non-filer tax are common causes.

How do I estimate my IESCO bill?

Use the IESCO calculator and enter your monthly units and consumer type.
