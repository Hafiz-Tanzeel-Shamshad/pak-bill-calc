MEPCO Bill Calculator 2026 - Multan and Bahawalpur Tariff Guide

By Hafiz Tanzeel Shamshad — Updated: May 20, 2026

MEPCO is one of the largest DISCOs in Pakistan, covering a wide region in South Punjab. This guide explains how MEPCO bills are calculated, where to check your bill online, and how to estimate your monthly charges using NEPRA 2026 slab rates. If you want to calculate immediately, open the MEPCO bill calculator and enter your units.

MEPCO follows the national NEPRA tariff slabs, so your bill depends largely on your unit consumption and consumer type. Knowing which slab you fall into helps you control costs and avoid sudden jumps.

MEPCO coverage areas

MEPCO covers Multan, Bahawalpur, Dera Ghazi Khan, Muzaffargarh, Rahim Yar Khan, Vehari, Khanewal, and Lodhran. If your bill shows MEPCO as the DISCO, you can use the calculator on this site or the main calculator for a general estimate.

How MEPCO bills are calculated

MEPCO bills are based on NEPRA 2026 slab rates. Your consumer type (lifeline, protected, or non-protected) determines which slab rates are used. After energy charges, fixed charges and taxes like GST and income tax are added. Finally, surcharges like the Neelum Jhelum and financing cost charges are included. The FCA line item can change monthly and is often the reason two similar bills differ.

How to check MEPCO bill online

Use the PITC portal to check your MEPCO bill online. Enter the 14 digit reference number and the bill will appear. You can download the PDF or print it. If you want a step by step tutorial, see our online bill checking guide.

Why your MEPCO bill is high

The most common reason is a slab jump. If you cross 200 or 300 units, the rate rises sharply. Seasonal usage, especially cooling, can push you into higher slabs. FCA changes can also raise the total even if units do not change much. Use the slab chart to see the exact rate jumps.

Tips to reduce MEPCO costs

Track your unit usage weekly if possible. Replace old appliances with efficient alternatives, limit peak hour usage, and keep AC temperature at a moderate level. If your roof is suitable, explore solar as a long term solution. The solar savings calculator can help you estimate payback.

MEPCO complaints and support

For billing complaints or meter issues, contact MEPCO customer service or visit your local office. If you believe the reading is wrong, file a complaint quickly and keep the complaint number. You can also use our complaint guide for a full checklist.

FAQs

Which areas are covered by MEPCO?

MEPCO covers Multan, Bahawalpur, Dera Ghazi Khan, Muzaffargarh, and nearby districts.

How can I check my MEPCO bill online?

Use the PITC portal with your reference number to view or download the bill.

Is the MEPCO tariff different from NEPRA slabs?

MEPCO generally follows NEPRA national slabs with standard surcharges.

Why did my MEPCO bill increase suddenly?

Common reasons include slab jumps, higher usage, and monthly FCA changes.

What is the fastest way to estimate my MEPCO bill?

Use the MEPCO calculator and enter your units, consumer type, and tax status.
