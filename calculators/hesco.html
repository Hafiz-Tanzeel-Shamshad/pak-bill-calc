About HESCO Billing in 2026

HESCO (Hyderabad Electric Supply Company) serves Hyderabad, Mirpurkhas, Sanghar, Badin, and nearby areas in Sindh. Billing is based on NEPRA 2026 slab rates, which means each block of units carries a different price. As consumption rises, the marginal rate increases, so keeping monthly units below the next slab can protect your budget.

The calculator above applies those slabs and then adds standard charges that appear on HESCO bills, including GST, income tax by filer status, fixed charges by sanctioned load, and per-unit surcharges like the Neelum Jhelum and financing cost charges. The FCA toggle is included because fuel adjustments change each month and often move the total up or down.

If your official bill is higher than expected, compare the units with your meter and look for slab jumps or reading errors. HESCO customers can also check bills online via PITC or visit their local customer service office. To reduce long term costs, focus on efficient appliances and consider rooftop solar if your roof space is sufficient for net metering.

Frequently Asked Questions about HESCO Bills

How do I pay my HESCO bill?

You can pay your HESCO bill through online banking, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or at partner banks like HBL, NBP, and MCB branches in Sindh. The PITC portal also accepts card payments for online settlement.

What is the HESCO helpline number?

HESCO's customer helpline is 118 for billing issues and fault reports. You can also visit HESCO offices in Hyderabad, Mirpurkhas, or Nawabshah for in-person assistance with your account.

Does HESCO support net metering?

Yes, HESCO processes net metering applications for solar installations. Submit your system design, equipment specifications, and property documents to your local HESCO office to apply.

How can I check my HESCO bill online?

Visit bill.pitc.com.pk and enter your 14-digit reference number to view, download, or print your HESCO bill. The service is free and available 24 hours a day.

Why is my HESCO bill higher than the calculator estimate?

The calculator uses NEPRA's 2026 base slab rates only. Your actual bill may be higher due to FCA changes, QTA adjustments, arrears, or penalty charges not included in this estimate.
