All ten DISCO calculators on this page use the same NEPRA 2026 tariff framework, ensuring consistent and comparable estimates across different regions. Whether you live in Lahore served by LESCO, in Multan served by MEPCO, or in Peshawar served by PESCO, the underlying calculation logic is identical, making it easy to compare bills if you are moving between cities or managing multiple properties in different DISCO areas. Each calculator applies the official slab rates for residential, commercial, and industrial consumer categories.

Your actual electricity bill can differ from the calculator estimate for several reasons beyond the base tariff. Monthly Fuel Cost Adjustment varies based on global fuel prices and the generation mix used by your DISCO in a given month. Quarterly Tariff Adjustments are applied by NEPRA to recover or refund capacity charges. Late payment surcharges, meter rent, and TV fees are also added to your bill. Our calculator includes typical values for these items, but your actual charges may vary slightly depending on your DISCO's current adjustments.

Understanding the difference between lifeline, protected, and non-protected consumer categories is important for accurate bill estimation. Lifeline consumers who use 50 units or less per month receive the highest subsidy and pay the lowest per-unit rate. Protected consumers with monthly usage up to 200 units also benefit from subsidized tariffs that are significantly lower than the full slab rates. Non-protected and commercial consumers pay the standard NEPRA-approved tariff, which increases progressively across consumption slabs from 1 to over 700 units per month.

Each DISCO in Pakistan follows the same NEPRA tariff framework but may have minor differences in how certain surcharges are applied. For example, some DISCOs have higher distribution losses that can affect the FCA calculation, while others may have different timelines for implementing quarterly adjustments. The individual DISCO calculator pages include region-specific notes about local billing practices and customer support contacts. If you are unsure which DISCO serves your area, check your latest electricity bill for the company name and logo printed at the top.
