Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I am a protected consumer?

If your average monthly units over the last three months are 200 units or less, you are typically treated as protected. Read our protected consumer guide for full details on benefits and slab rates.

What is FCA in the electricity bill?

FCA is the Fuel Cost Adjustment. It changes monthly based on fuel prices and generation costs. Learn more in our FCA guide.

How is GST calculated on the bill?

GST is applied as a percentage of energy charges before other surcharges are added.

Why is my bill higher than last month?

Higher units, slab jumps, FCA changes, or non-filer tax rates can raise your total even if usage is similar.

How can I reduce my electricity bill?

Shift heavy loads to off-peak, improve appliance efficiency, and consider solar where possible.

Understanding Your Bill

This Pakistan electricity bill calculator is built around NEPRA 2026 base tariff rates and the latest public surcharge details. The calculation first applies the slab rate based on your selected consumer type. For lifeline and protected consumers, the first slabs are cheaper, while non-protected slabs rise sharply after 200 units. View the complete breakdown in our tariff slabs guide.

After the energy charge is calculated, we add fixed charges based on your sanctioned load. The minimum monthly charge is Rs 600 for loads below 5 kW and Rs 2,000 for loads at or above 5 kW. We then add GST, estimated income tax based on filer status, and per-unit surcharges like the Neelum Jhelum and financing cost charges. The FCA toggle helps you include a recent Fuel Cost Adjustment so your estimate is closer to current bills.

This website is designed for households who want a quick estimate before the official bill arrives. The estimate is not a replacement for your DISCO bill, but it is a practical way to plan your budget. If you are consistently surprised by your total, check for slab jumps, inaccurate meter readings, or non-filer tax. Learn why your bill is high or find out which appliances use the most electricity. You can also explore our solar savings calculator to see how much monthly cost could be reduced with a small solar system, or read our solar worth it analysis.

This calculator provides an estimated electricity bill based on NEPRA's 2026 base tariff rates. Your actual bill may differ due to monthly Fuel Cost Adjustment (FCA), Quarterly Tariff Adjustment (QTA), local surcharges, and meter reading variations.
