Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

PakBillCalc respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how long we retain it, and how third-party services may store information when you use our site.

Information we collect

We collect minimal personal information. If you contact us via the contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. We do not collect or store billing information, physical addresses, or payment details.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and measure site performance. Cookies may be set by our analytics and advertising partners. You can control cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner. For more information about cookie choice options, visit cookiechoices.org, which provides resources for managing your cookie preferences across different websites and advertising networks.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. Analytics data may include pages visited, time spent, device type, browser version, and approximate geographic location. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. Data is retained for 14 months before automatic deletion.

Google AdSense and third-party advertising

When ads are enabled, Google AdSense and other third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party cookies used for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

We do not control which cookies are set by third-party ad networks. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of our advertising partners for more information.

Information you provide

If you contact us through the form or via email, we may store your name, email address, and message content so we can respond. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

Contact form submissions are retained for a maximum of 12 months. Analytics data is retained for 14 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data: the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data; the right to restrict or object to processing; and the right to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pakbillcalc.online.

Third-party data sharing

We share anonymized usage data with Google Analytics and, when ads are enabled, with Google AdSense. We do not share personally identifiable information with any third party except where required by law.

Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no transmission over the internet is completely secure. Use the site at your own discretion.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services change or as legal requirements evolve. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
